Study Skills

Introduction

This website will help you with all of your schooling needs. It contains many useful tips and tricks alongside some good advice so that you can pass your tests confidently, get consistently great marks on your assignments, improve your exam scores, and minimise stress.
You can click on the images or section titles to go to the page for each topic.

Homework

Homework can get very stressful at times and it is important to know how to manage it. If you leave homework until the last minute, you can get overwhelmed, so it is important to stay organised. This website will show you how to get all your homework done on time.

Assignments and Projects

Assignments are used to assess you on how well you understand a topic. You will usually have an amount of time to work on them at home, and they contribute to most of your marks for a subject. It is important to start assignments and projects early.

Tests and Exams

We all know how difficult it is to do tests, whether it comes to studying, practicing, time management or controlling stress. It is not easy, but you can learn how to get good at it. Here you will find some skills you need to know when preparing for a test, how to stress down before a test and tips for when you actually need to do your test.

Mental Health

When you are tying to study, whether it be working on assignments or studying for a test, it always really important to stay on top of your mental health. In here you will find lots of different tips and tricks to help you with your mental health; these are easy and simple ways to make sure that you are mentally fit and able to complete work on your own with no problems.
